Privacy Policy

Onhouse Technologies Pvt Ltd ("we", "our", or "us") respects your privacy and is committed to protecting the personal information you share with us through our website, mobile application, and related services ("Services").

Information We Collect

We collect personal information only when it is necessary to provide our Services. This may include:

  • Basic personal details such as name, mobile number, email address
  • Identity information required for KYC (such as PAN and Aadhaar, where applicable)
  • Transaction and wallet activity
  • Device, usage, and technical information (such as IP address and app interactions)
All information is collected through lawful means, with your knowledge and consent.

How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide, operate, and improve our Services
  • Complete KYC and comply with legal and regulatory obligations
  • Process payments, wallet activity, and transfers
  • Communicate important service-related updates
  • Maintain security and prevent fraud

Data Retention and Security

We retain personal information only for as long as necessary to provide our Services or to comply with applicable laws and regulations.
We use commercially reasonable safeguards to protect your data from loss, misuse, unauthorized access, disclosure, or alteration.

Sharing of Information

We do not sell or publicly disclose your personal information.
Your information may be shared only with:

  • RBI-regulated banking, wallet, and payment partners
  • KYC and verification service providers
  • Government or regulatory authorities, where required by law
All third parties are required to handle data securely and lawfully.

Third-Party Services and Links

Our Services may contain links to third-party websites or services not operated by us. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of such third parties and encourage you to review their respective privacy policies.

Refusal of Information

You may choose to refuse providing certain personal information. However, doing so may limit your ability to access some features or services.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Request deletion of your personal data, subject to legal and regulatory requirements
  • Request restriction or objection to certain processing activities
  • Request data portability, where applicable
We will respond to valid requests within the timelines prescribed by law.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.
